Zakariya Isah Zakariya

Director of Programs

Zakariya Isah Zakariya is a dynamic IT professional and seasoned leader with over 10 years of cross-sector expertise in administrative management, IT systems, and community development. As the Program Director at Khadija Brahim Foundation (Abuja), he spearheads WASH initiatives and climate adaptation projects, including biodigester toilets and sustainable water systems, to bolster community resilience. His role encompasses strategic budget management, donor relations, policy advocacy, and fostering partnerships for scalable impact. Prior to nonprofit leadership, Zakariya honed his operational acumen in the construction industry, serving as an Administrative Specialist for firms like TECNIS EPC International Ltd, H&S Nigeria Ltd, and NEIMS. He optimized HR, procurement, logistics, and IT workflows, demonstrating a sharp ability to streamline complex systems. Education & Expertise: MSc in Information Technology (in progress) | BSc in Computer Science (NOUN) Technical proficiency: Web Development (HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React, PHP) and data systems Passionate about leveraging technology and leadership to drive sustainable development.
